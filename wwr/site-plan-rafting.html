Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating this website for our rafting company is to be able to reach a wider audience of people. We want to be able to provide a page that helps people learn about our trips and the different and unique experience we offer. Having this website can also help us build our brand and be recognized in different parts of the world. In that way, we will be able to build revenue for our business in a more current way.

Audience

Our target audience is people looking to live new experiences that take them out of their day to day. They are people who enjoy being outdoors and admire nature. This group of people includes families, young adults, and groups of friends. This website's content, colors, fonts, and general design would be oriented to this group of people, using colors that evoke a feeling of adventure and exploration and with quality images that show our services. This group of people wants to connect more with nature and live new experiences. My audience will connect to this site through laptops or desktops as they would like a quiet place to read all the information they need to purchase any of our services.
